## Aircraft

### CV-5 Kestrel
The Kestrel is a light cargo aircraft capable of carrying decently sized loads such as palletes and small vehicles

It is notorious for having possibly one of the worst flight controllers ever made

### CV-7 Merlin
The Merlin (also referred to by many as the Kestrel 2) is a significantly more capable aircraft than its predecessor, featuring engines that are many times more powerful and efficient, and an actually functioning (and quite good) flight control system
